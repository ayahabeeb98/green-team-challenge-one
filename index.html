About

I’m a chef with 10 years experience cooking in my client’s homes. My journey started years earlier when I began cooking elaborate family meals on the holidays, turning into a true passion that I live and breathe.

For me, cooking is a transformational experience, combining the scents, sounds and tastes of complex flavors of different regions and cultures. Allow me to share with you my broad knowledge of some of these unique and special cooking techniques that I have learned under world-renowned chefs.
