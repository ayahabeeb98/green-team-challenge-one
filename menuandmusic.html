My Menu & Music

Welcome to my featured selection of menu options. Don’t worry if you’re not sure which one to choose; I can work with you to select a cuisine, or even a mix of cuisines, based on your dream dining experience.

Cooking in silence is like watching a Broadway show, with no singing. Here are the playlists I use to get in the cooking groove. Play them in your kitchen and add some fire to your cooking process.

American BBQ

For some of us in New York, nothing beats a barbequed rack of ribs. Experience bold and smoky flavors, with a range of meats and chicken options.

Italian

Get a taste of this Mediterranean cuisine consisting of ingredients, recipes, and cooking techniques developed across the Italian peninsula since antiquity.

Mexican

An evening that will make you forget that you're not in Baja California. Go beyond the taco to experience the Mesoamerican roots of this cuisine.

Indian

An intoxicating blend of herbs, fragrances, and spices will turn up the heat as we celebrate the rich diversity and history of this South Asian cuisine.

Asian

Dishes inspired by Thai, Chinese, Vietnamese, and Indonesian classics. Take a delicious tour of Asia from the comfort of your dining table.
